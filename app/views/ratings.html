Ratings

Ratings found here belong to horses who have raced at least 5 times at Gulfstream Park since December 2012, a total of {{ horses.length }} horses. Horses who have raced fewer than 5 times have also been handicapped but aren't presented, they should be uploaded in time, but fear that amount of data (~7000 horses) will slow the app significantly.

The ratings are based on the performance of the horse in the type/class of race the horse competed in, a full explanation can be fonud in the Handicapping Method tab below. In time I will post regular blogs that walk through the handicapping process. The ratings are a work in progress, improvements will (hopefully) be made over time (as I continue to learn).

To see the ratings click on the Gulfstream Ratings tab, which will present a small table with the available horses, which can be paginated through or filtered using the search tool. To view a horse's form and ratings click on the corresponding Go to Form show link
