Blog

In this corner of the web app you will find various posts. Under the Handicapping header will be individual races and the calculation of ratings for that race. The Race Previews/Reviews will look at individual races, and under the Misc header will be a variety of posts, hopefully including a few interactive visualisations, and tools. Some of the handicapping posts should also serve as mini-vignettes to the RcappeR package. Each post under the Handicapping header (and most of the Race Previews/Reviews) will be written using the rmarkdown package.

The TRUE statements occasionally seen at the top of a blog post is just the results of querying my MongoDB for race data.
